How do I use Kaffy?
Place one pouch between your gum and upper lip. Let it sit for 15–30 minutes — you'll feel the lift within 5–10 minutes. When you're done, remove and discard. Do not chew or swallow the pouch.
How will it make me feel?
Calm, focused, switched on — not wired. The L-Theanine takes the edge off the caffeine, and Cognizin® supports sustained mental clarity. Most people feel "an optimized version of themselves" rather than a stimulant rush.
Does Kaffy contain nicotine or tobacco?
No. Kaffy is 100% nicotine-free and tobacco-free. It's a nootropic pouch — clean caffeine plus brain-supporting ingredients.
How many pouches can I take per day?
We recommend up to 3 pouches per day. Don't exceed that. Avoid taking one within 6 hours of bed if you're caffeine-sensitive.
When will my order arrive?
Orders ship within 1–2 business days. Standard delivery in the continental US is 3–5 business days. Free shipping on orders over $40 and on all subscriptions.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes — skip, pause, or cancel from your account at any time. No hoops, no calls.
Will it affect my gums?
Kaffy pouches use premium, non-irritating materials designed for everyday use. As with any oral product, rotate placement and discontinue if you notice irritation.
